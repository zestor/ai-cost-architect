Purpose

Tier decision

Portfolio Intake is the tier decision. Assess three axes: annual spend, regulatory risk, and organizational readiness. The output of intake is a tier assignment (T1, T2, T3) that determines how much of the 21-artifact pack applies.

  • Tier 1 — Annual AI spend > $5M, or SR 11-7 tiered, or public-facing safety risk. All 21 artifacts.
  • Tier 2 — Annual spend $500K–$5M with moderate risk. Core pack (14 artifacts).
  • Tier 3 — Under $500K, low risk. Lightweight pack (7 artifacts).
