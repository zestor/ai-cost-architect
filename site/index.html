    What this framework is for

    You have been asked to bring order to a growing portfolio of enterprise AI use cases — each with different owners, different risk profiles, different regulators, and none with defensible unit economics. This framework tells you what to do next, in what order, with what artifact, and why.

    The methodology in seven sentences
    1. Decide whether to take the project and at what tier (Phase 0).
    2. Get a signed scope with five named owners (Phase 1).
    3. Decompose the workload, then bound the cost with defensible unit prices (Phase 2).
    4. Instrument production and reconcile to invoice within ±10% (Phase 3).
    5. Build unit economics, componentized forecast, and scenarios (Phase 4).
    6. Ship a business case with an attributable-benefits page (Phase 5 and Phase 6).
    7. Hand off the operating model so the framework survives you (Phase 7).
